Platform Expertise

Mastery of the Industry-Leading Identity Ecosystems

While our engineering principles are universal, our deep-tier technical mastery is focused on the platforms that define modern enterprise security. We don't just implement these tools; we optimize them to their full mathematical potential.

SailPoint Mastery

We specialize in the full SailPoint IdentityIQ and IdentityNow suites. Our expertise includes:

  • Lifecycle Event Management: Precision-tuned Joiner, Mover, and Leaver (JML) processes.
  • Identity Cube Optimization: Ensuring a 360-degree view of user access with zero data redundancy.
  • Advanced Rule Writing: Custom Java-based BeanShell rules for complex business logic.

Saviynt Specialization

We leverage Saviynt's Enterprise Identity Cloud to provide converged security solutions:

  • Cloud-Native Governance: Seamless integration with AWS, Azure, and GCP environments.
  • SOD (Segregation of Duties): Building automated, real-time risk analysis and remediation frameworks.
  • External Identity Management: Securely managing third-party, vendor, and bot identities.
